Beef Suya

Description"

Embark on a culinary journey with our tantalizing Nigerian Beef Suya, a street food sensation that captivates with bold flavors and a smoky aroma. Thin slices of beef are expertly coated in Yaji, a spice blend of ground peanuts, chili pepper, and aromatic spices, creating a symphony of taste that dances on the palate. Grilled to perfection, the succulent beef imparts a smoky char, enhancing the complexity of flavors. Served with sliced onions and tomatoes, each bite of our Beef Suya is a celebration of Nigeria's vibrant street food culture—bold, unforgettable, and irresistibly delicious.

Ingredients

  • 500g thinly sliced beef
  • 1/2 cup ground peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons chili pepper
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Skewers for grilling
  • Sliced onions and tomatoes for serving

Steps on Preparation

  1. In a bowl, mix ground peanuts, chili pepper, ginger, paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, and salt to create the Yaji spice blend.
  2. Thread thinly sliced beef onto skewers and generously coat with the Yaji spice blend.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the skewers, turning occasionally, until the beef is cooked to your desired doneness with a smoky char.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with sliced onions and tomatoes.
  6. Enjoy the bold and smoky perfection of Nigerian Beef Suya—a street food favorite that delights the senses.
